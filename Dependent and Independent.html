Dependent and Independent Variables

1: Relationship between study time and test scores
- Independent variable: Study time (in hours)
- Dependent variable: Test scores (in percentage or grade)

2: Relationship between dosage and pain relief
- Independent variable: Dosage of a pain medication (in milligrams)
- Dependent variable: Level of pain relief (measured on a scale, such as 1-10)

3: Relationship between the number of dish that the chef will cook and the time it will spent on cooking.
- Independent variable: Dish the chef will cook. (number of dish)
- Dependent variable: Time spent on cooking. (in hours/minutes)
